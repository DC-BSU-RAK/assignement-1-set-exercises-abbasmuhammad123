Overview

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror drama television series created by the Duffer Brothers for Netflix. Set in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, in the 1980s, the series combines supernatural elements with nostalgic references to 1980s pop culture.

The series premiered on Netflix on July 15, 2016, and has become one of the streaming platform's flagship shows. Known for its compelling storytelling, strong performances, and authentic 1980s aesthetic, Stranger Things has received critical acclaim and developed a dedicated fanbase worldwide.

The series' success lies in its ability to blend supernatural horror with coming-of-age stories, government conspiracies, and heartfelt character development, all wrapped in a nostalgic package that pays homage to the works of Steven Spielberg, John Carpenter, Stephen King, and other iconic creators from the 1980s.
